# ADR-0006: One-click /evaluate reconciled with human sign-off

**Status:** Accepted

## Context
A one-click "evaluate everything" path is wanted for demo speed, but a mandatory
mid-pipeline human review gate (after extraction) conflicts with running straight through —
and human-in-the-loop is a core principle.

## Decision
`/evaluate` runs end-to-end without stopping and produces a decision marked `AI_PROPOSED`
(logging `REVIEW_SKIPPED`). The mandatory human touch moves to a **final sign-off**:
`/decision/approve` sets `HUMAN_APPROVED`; export is gated on it.

## Consequences
+ Fast one-click demo AND preserved human authority + auditability.
+ Audit trail always distinguishes an autonomous proposal from an approved decision.
- Requires a `review_status` field and an approve step in the flow.

## Alternatives considered
- Pure bypass (no distinction): rejected — breaks auditability/human-in-the-loop.
- Hard mid-pipeline stop: rejected — kills the one-click path.
